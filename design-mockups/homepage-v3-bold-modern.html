The problem with sourcing from China remotely

Alibaba and trade platforms show you polished profiles — not reality. Factories exaggerate capabilities, hide quality issues, and outsource to unvetted subcontractors. By the time you discover the truth, you've already lost time and money.

We eliminate the guesswork by visiting every factory in person. Our bilingual team walks the production floor, interviews management, and documents everything — giving you the unvarnished truth before you commit.
