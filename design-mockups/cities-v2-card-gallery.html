Did You Know

China's supply chain by the numbers

🧦

1/3 of the world's socks

Come from one town — Zhuji, Zhejiang. A single city produces over 30% of global sock production.

🀄

80% of mahjong tiles

Dongyang, a small Zhejiang city, dominates global mahjong tile manufacturing along with magnetic toys.

🔌

60,000+ electronics stalls

Huaqiangbei in Shenzhen is the world's largest electronics market — bigger than Akihabara and Yongsan combined.

🪑

3 million sqm of furniture

Lecong, Foshan is the world's largest furniture wholesale market with 180+ showroom buildings.

🏪

5 months to see it all

Yiwu's market has 75,000+ booths. At 1 minute per booth, 8 hours a day, it takes over 5 months to see every stall.

☕

95% of Chinese coffee

Grown in Yunnan. Most people don't know China even produces coffee — it's the 14th largest producer globally.

🏷️

$0.05 per unit saved

Specialist factory towns like Yongkang can beat generalist factory prices by 30-40% through hyper-specialization.

🌏

50%+ global share

Some Chinese factory towns produce over half the world's supply of their specialized product category.
