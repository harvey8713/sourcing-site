About This Trip

Shenzhen is the world's electronics manufacturing capital — home to Huaqiangbei, the largest electronics market on earth, and thousands of factories producing everything from IoT sensors to consumer devices. In 5 intensive days, we take you inside the factories that power the global hardware supply chain.

This sprint is designed for product managers, hardware founders, and importers who want more than an Alibaba search — they want to shake hands with factory owners, walk production lines, and leave with a verified shortlist of manufacturing partners.

What's Included

Pre-trip supplier shortlist of 10-15 qualified factories
On-site audits at 5-8 facilities with production line walkthroughs
Guided tour of Huaqiangbei electronics market with sourcing engineer
Sample collection, testing coordination, and shipping arrangement
30+ page due diligence report with photos, videos, and supplier rankings
Post-trip supplier negotiation and contract review support
