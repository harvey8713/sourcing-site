Electronics Sourcing Shenzhen, Guangdong 5 Days

Shenzhen Electronics Sprint

5-day deep dive into Huaqiangbei, OEM factories, and the Shenzhen hardware ecosystem with an experienced bilingual sourcing team.

Overview

Shenzhen is the world's electronics manufacturing capital. In 5 days, we take you inside the factories that build everything from IoT devices to consumer electronics. You'll walk Huaqiangbei's legendary component markets, audit potential manufacturing partners, and leave with a verified shortlist of suppliers ready to produce.

This sprint is designed for hardware startups, product managers, and importers who need more than an Alibaba search — they need boots on the ground in China's most dynamic manufacturing ecosystem.
