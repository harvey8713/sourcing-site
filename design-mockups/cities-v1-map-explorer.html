Shenzhen

Electronics Manufacturing Capital

Why Shenzhen Matters

Shenzhen is the undisputed global capital of electronics manufacturing. Home to Huaqiangbei — the world's largest electronics market with 60,000+ stalls — and thousands of factories producing IoT devices, consumer electronics, and PCBs. If your product has a circuit board, it was probably prototyped here.

Guangzhou

Textile & Consumer Goods Hub

Why Guangzhou Matters

Guangzhou is China's southern trade gateway — home to the Canton Fair (world's largest trade show), the Zhongda fabric market, and sprawling specialty wholesale districts for bags, watches, toys, and fashion. It's where global apparel brands source their next season's collection.

Yiwu

World's Largest Wholesale Market

Why Yiwu Matters

Yiwu International Trade City spans 4 million square meters with 75,000+ booths — if you spent 1 minute at each booth, 8 hours a day, it would take over 5 months to see everything. This is where Amazon sellers, dollar stores, and global importers source everything from Christmas decorations to kitchen gadgets.

Dongguan

Precision Manufacturing Belt

Why Dongguan Matters

Dongguan is the precision heart of the Pearl River Delta — mold-making, CNC machining, and industrial electronics. The factories here make the tools that make everything else. If Shenzhen designs it, Dongguan manufactures it.

Foshan

World's Furniture Capital

Why Foshan Matters

Foshan produces 70% of China's furniture exports. The Lecong wholesale market spans 3 million sqm with 180+ buildings. From solid wood MCM-style cabinets to contemporary upholstered sofas — the entire furniture supply chain lives here.
